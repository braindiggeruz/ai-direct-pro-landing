Other findings:

- **Average RU article ≈ 605 words** — thin by 2026 standards for competitive queries; pillar candidates should be 1200–1800 words.
- 🔴 `razrabotka-ai-bota-v-tashkente-cena` — body is about e-commerce benefits, not development price in Tashkent (slug/intent mismatch; also flagged in technical audit).
- **0 of 61 articles contain a comparison table** (renderer supports no `table` block type; only `list` is available). 15 articles have no list block at all. AI answer engines strongly prefer tabular/structured comparisons — emulate with structured `list` blocks or add a `table` block type to the renderer.
- 4 drafts pending: `ai-bot-dlya-internet-magazina-zadachi`, `ai-bot-dlya-kliniki-zadachi`, `ai-bot-dlya-salona-krasoty-zadachi`, `ai-bot-dlya-uchebnogo-tsentra-zadachi` (RU only).
- `topicCluster` values are inconsistent: mix of slugs (`telegram-bot-dlya-biznesa`), free-text Russian («чат-бот для бизнеса Ташкент»), and one-off labels — 37 distinct "clusters" for 40 RU articles means clustering is effectively unused. Normalize to a fixed slug taxonomy.

## 2.3 Internal linking audit

- Blog → money: ✅ every article has `targetMoneyPage` + 3–6 `internalLinks` to service pages.
- Money → blog: service pages render a "Полезные статьи / Foydali maqolalar" grid, but **48 of 61 articles are never explicitly linked from any page's `internalLinks`** — they rely only on the blog index and automatic related-article widgets. High-value articles (pricing, ROI, Payme/Click) should be hand-linked from their money pages.
- `/blog/[slug]` unprefixed URLs 404 (technical audit) — any legacy links to them are dead.
