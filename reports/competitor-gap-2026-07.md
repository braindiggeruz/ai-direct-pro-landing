## Key takeaways

1. **Case studies are the single biggest gap** — every strong competitor has them; AI assistants cite numeric case content disproportionately.
2. **DIY/how-to funnel is missing** — "как создать бота самому" queries feed competitors' funnels; gptbot.uz can capture them and convert to done-for-you service.
3. **Uncontested verticals** (logistics, construction/real-estate leads, delivery) have zero RU-language Uzbekistan-focused coverage anywhere — fastest possible rankings.
4. Defend moats: Uzbek language, Payme/Click/Humo, geo pages — competitors cannot follow without local presence.
