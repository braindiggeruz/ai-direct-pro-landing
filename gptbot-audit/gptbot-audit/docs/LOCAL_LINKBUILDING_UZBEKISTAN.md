## Tier 3 — Telegram seeding (risk: medium, requires owner approval)

Telegram is the dominant channel in Uzbekistan; native posts in
**relevant** RU/UZ channels drive 5–10× more clicks per impression than
banner ads. Use only channels with > 5 000 organic followers and topics
matching B2B / SMB owners.

| Channel theme                         | Suggested post angle                                          | Target URL                     | Risk    |
|---------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|--------------------------------|---------|

| Marketing / SMM Uzbekistan            | "Как Instagram Direct превращается в воронку"                  | `/ru/ai-menedzher-dlya-instagram/` | medium |

| HoReCa Tashkent                       | "Стол бронируется ночью — администратор подтверждает утром"    | `/ru/ai-bot-dlya-horeca/`      | medium  |
| Uzbek-language entrepreneurs          | "Telegram bot biznes uchun — qanday ishlaydi"                  | `/uz/biznes-uchun-ai-bot/`     | medium  |

Rules:
- One post per channel per month max — burst posting flags spam.
- Always include a clear value (demo link, sample dialog screenshot),
  never a bare backlink.
- Do not pay for posts that hide the `#реклама` / `#reklama` tag — it
  violates local advertising law and Telegram TOS.

| **Repost.uz**    | Hands-on test of AI vs human first-contact response times                      | `/ru/avtomatizatsiya-zayavok/`      |

Pitch rules:
- One outlet at a time. Wait 5 working days before pitching the same
  story to a second outlet.
- Always offer a concrete, verifiable claim (response time numbers,
  scenario screenshots). Editors reject vague pitches.
- Never offer payment for placement — risks de-indexing and ruins the
  long-term relationship.

## Priority order (recommended)

1. Tier 1 directories (week 1) — fastest, lowest risk.
2. GBP + Yandex Business + Yandex Webmaster region setup (week 1).
3. Telegram seeding (week 2–3) — highest direct conversion.
4. Tier 2 catalogs (week 2–4) — slow but durable links.
5. PR pitch + Tier 4 partnerships (week 4+) — biggest links, longest
   cycle.

## Tracking

- Every backlink: log in a spreadsheet with referring URL, anchor,
  target URL, date placed, do-follow / no-follow, DA estimate.
- Use Bing Webmaster `Backlinks` and Yandex Webmaster `Внешние ссылки`
  weekly — Google Search Console lags 2-4 weeks on this.
- Drop any partnership that produces only no-follow placements on
  low-quality directories. Quality > quantity.
