## Rules (do not break)

- Real placements only. **No paid link farms, no PBNs, no DR-x guarantees.**
- No anchor-text spam — vary anchors, prefer brand + descriptive variants.
- Don’t post the same article verbatim across many sites — Google treats this
  as syndication and discounts it.
- Submit to **one source per week per niche** at most, to avoid being seen as
  a coordinated link drop.
- Track every placement in a private sheet so we can react if any of them
  decide to nofollow or remove later.

## Expected outcome

- Indexing: faster Bing/Yandex/Yandex.Uzbekistan discovery.
- Authority: from near-zero Site Explorer to a small but real local profile
  in 4–8 weeks (no overnight DR jumps — anything claiming that is fraud).
- Leads: secondary benefit from the local-language posts and Telegram cross-posts.
